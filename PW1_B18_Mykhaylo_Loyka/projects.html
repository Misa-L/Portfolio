Projects

Sourbay

This is my friend’s music streaming platform, which is designed to be very similar to Spotify in its functionality and user experience. I personally implemented a sophisticated algorithm using ASP.NET Core, which allows for efficient processing and analysis of large amounts of song data. The algorithm works by first collecting song information, such as genre, lyrics, energy level, tempo, mood, and many other features—over 500 in total. Then, it analyzes this data using various methods, such as pattern recognition and feature weighting, to create detailed song profiles and recommendations. This system can automatically suggest songs that match a listener’s preferences, mood, or listening history, making the platform feel highly personalized and dynamic. Our development team is constantly looking for ways to optimize the database performance to ensure that music playback remains seamless even under heavy server load. My friend is responsible for working on both the frontend and backend development of the homepage. He also provides guidance and teaches a lot about animations, backend functions, and integrating various features into the website. Meanwhile, other team members contribute by working on different parts of the platform, including the music bar, user login and registration systems, and overall design elements, ensuring that both functionality and user experience are smooth and attractive. This is still the very beginning of the project, but we are working hard, constantly improving the platform, and I am very optimistic that we will eventually complete it successfully.
